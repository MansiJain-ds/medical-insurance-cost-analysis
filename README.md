# Medical Insurance Cost Analysis

## Objective
To analyze medical insurance data and identify key factors affecting insurance charges.

## Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Key Insights
- Smokers incur significantly higher medical charges.
- Medical expenses increase with age.
- Higher BMI contributes to increased insurance cost.
- Smoking is the most influential factor affecting medical charges.

## Conclusion
Lifestyle factors such as smoking and BMI strongly impact medical insurance costs.
